Artist Statement

I try as much as possible to express myself in my artworks. I make use of vibrant and bright colours to portray emotions, mood, feelings and also depict daily hustle as an entrepreneur and also a visual Artist. My art form is experimental, I work with different mediums (oil, acrylic, pastel, charcoal, pencil and etc) to tell a story. I use cool, warm, colourful colours a to depict my subject matter.
